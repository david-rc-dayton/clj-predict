clj-predict.solar

day-of-year

(day-of-year date)

Calculate the number of days elapsed since the beginning of the year. Takes a java.util.Date object for the starting time as its only argument.

Returns a floating point representation of elapsed time in the UTC timezone.

solar-altitude

(solar-altitude date)

Calculate the distance between the Earth and the Sun. Takes a java.util.Date object as its only argument.

Returns the Sun’s altitude at the given time, in meters.

solar-latitude

(solar-latitude date)

Calculate the latitude of the Sun. Takes a java.util.Date object as its only argument.

Returns the Sun’s latitude at the given time, in degrees.

solar-longitude

(solar-longitude date)

Calculate the longitude of the Sun. Takes a java.util.Date object as its only argument.

Returns the Sun’s longitude at the given time, in degrees.

solar-position

(solar-position date)

Calculate the location of the Sun relative to the Earth. Takes a java.util.Date object as its only argument.

Returns a map containing the keys :lat :lon :alt of the Sun at the given time, in degrees and meters.

solar-properties

Orbital properties of the Earth relative to the Sun. Available keys for the returned solar-properties map are:

:mean-distance - Average distance of the Earth from the Sun, in meters
:eccentricity - Eccentricity of the Earth’s orbit around the Sun
:orbital-period - Duration of the Earth’s orbit around the Sun, in days
:declination - Solar elevation above the Equator at solstice, in degrees
